Welcome

Whether you are trying to understand the odds of you winning poker, or trying to predict market fluctuations, understanding basic probability is absolutely essential. This site was created to help you explore some fun probability problems through a simulator that will walk you step by step through the solving process. The Monty Hall problem and the Birthday problem are two problems that have surprising results that often seem unintuitive to new learners of probability.

The Monty Hall Problem
The Monty Hall Problem is a brain teaser that takes its origin in the game show Let's Make a Deal. This problem has stumped even some of the best mathematicians.
Stay or Switch. Which will you choose?
The Birthday Problem
How many people need to be in a room for it to be more likely than not that at least two people share a birthday? The answer is surprisingly low.
What's your guess?
Letter Mixup
If I give you a word, how many ways can you reorder the letters? How does this change if the word contains the same letter multiple times?
